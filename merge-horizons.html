PROJECT OVERVIEW


Merge Horizons blends the addictive fun of 2048 with creative village-building gameplay. Slide and merge items like shells, donuts, crowns, and stars to unlock new treasures, earn gold, and bring your village to life.

Core Features

  • Merge & Evolve: Combine matching items to discover new ones and boost your score.
  • Strategic Power-Ups: Reshuffle, undo moves, remove tiles, or swap items to master the board.
  • Build & Expand: Use earned gold to construct houses, shops, and landmarks â€” transforming your sleepy town into a thriving village.
  • Progress & Explore: Complete one village and move to the next, each with greater challenges and rewards.

Why It Stands Out

By fusing puzzle merging and town management, Merge Horizons offers endless replayability, relaxing strategy, and the satisfaction of watching your world grow one merge at a time.
