PROJECT OVERVIEW


Description

Educational game platform designed to teach children about space, ocean ecosystems, and wildlife through three engaging mini-games. The platform provides interactive learning experiences where children complete missions in different environments to learn important scientific concepts in an exciting and educational way.

Platform Mini-Games

  • Space Explorer: Players travel across the solar system, visiting planets and completing missions to learn about planetary characteristics, atmosphere, and space science.
  • Ocean Mysteries: Dive into underwater environments to explore marine life, coral reefs, and ocean ecosystems while completing conservation missions.
  • Wildlife Adventure: Navigate jungle environments, encounter animals and plants, and learn about ecosystem balance through nature-based challenges.

Project Ownership

  • Solo-developed educational game platform using Unity and C# with modular architecture for three distinct mini-games.
  • Designed, programmed, and produced all game systems, UI/UX, and educational content independently.

Architecture & Systems

  • Modular design with reusable assets across mini-games to reduce development time and maintain consistency.
  • Implemented event-driven architecture using ScriptableObject-based Event Channels for decoupled system communication.
  • Built a Service Locator system via ScriptableObjects to manage runtime services (save system, UI, audio).
  • Comprehensive localization system with dynamic text loading and language switching capabilities.
  • Local data storage to track mission progress, player achievements, and educational milestones across all mini-games.
  • Performance optimization ensures smooth operation on various mobile devices and platforms.

Educational Features

  • Interactive missions designed to teach scientific concepts through problem-solving and exploration.
  • Colorful, kid-friendly visual design with custom 2D sprites and animations tailored for children aged 6-12.
  • Simple yet intuitive user interface for easy navigation and accessibility.
  • Progress tracking system to monitor learning achievements and encourage continued engagement.
  • Multi-language support: Comprehensive localization system supporting multiple languages to reach global audiences.

Target Audience & Market Research

  • Primary Users: Children aged 6-12 seeking interactive learning experiences in science and nature.
  • Secondary Users: Parents, educators, and schools looking for engaging educational content.
  • Global Reach: Multi-language localization enables accessibility for international markets and diverse communities.
  • Addresses the growing market demand for educational games that combine entertainment with learning value.
  • Designed for digital platforms including mobile apps and web, adaptable for classroom and home environments.

Content Creation & Assets

  • Custom 2D sprites and animations created for three distinct environments: space, ocean, and jungle themes.
  • AI-assisted visual asset creation using tools like Leonardo AI and MidJourney for planets, marine life, and wildlife.
  • Generated immersive sound effects and background music with AI-driven audio synthesis tools.
  • Educational content research and implementation to ensure scientific accuracy and age-appropriate learning.
