PROJECT OVERVIEW


Bear Time is a charming village management game where players grow a small forest plot into a thriving bear town. Combining farming, crafting, cooking, and trading, it offers a relaxing yet strategic experience full of cute characters and rewarding progress.

Core Features

  • Build & Customize: Expand your village with farms, bakeries, factories, and cozy shops.
  • Farm & Produce: Grow crops, manage resources, and craft goods to fulfill orders.
  • Cook & Serve: Prepare meals and serve happy guests in your village café and restaurant.
  • Hire Animal Helpers: Recruit skilled animal workers to automate and optimize your workflow.
  • Trade & Explore: Complete local and global orders, mine for rare materials, and collect artifacts to decorate your village.

Why It Stands Out

With its mix of casual charm and deep management systems, Bear Time delivers the joy of building, creating, and nurturing a living world — perfect for players who love cozy strategy and simulation games.
